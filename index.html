Midjourney

Midjourney is an independent research lab focusing on developing advanced AI technologies, with a particular emphasis on creating AI models capable of generating highly detailed and creative visual art. Unlike language-based AI models like ChatGPT or Claude AI, Midjourney's primary focus is on visual arts and imagery.

The lab's notable work includes the development of AI systems that can generate images from textual descriptions. These systems utilize deep learning techniques to interpret written prompts and translate them into visual artworks, ranging from realistic images to more abstract and conceptual art.

Claude is currently in a limited free beta testing period. General public availability and pricing details have not been announced yet by Anthropic. It is expected to be a paid service or software when released.

Prices at Midjourney range from $10 to $120 depending on the plan.

Leonardo

Leonardo AI is an AI-driven platform specializing in creating hyper-realistic images and digital art using generative technology. It leverages deep learning and neural networks to produce visuals that closely mimic physical reality.

Primarily used for generating photorealistic images and digital art, it supports the creation of diverse digital assets like environments and concept art. It's equipped for rapid ideation and allows users to train their own AI models.

Offers a range from free access to subscription-based models and 150 credits that renew each day, with pricing plans up to about $60 per month, varying by features and services.

TensorArt

TensorArt is an AI image generator that simplifies the process of creating personalized AI-generated art. It's designed to help users explore and create unique artwork using various AI models and tools.

It offers an intuitive interface with a selection of AI models to create a wide variety of artistic outputs, including anime, super-realistic human figures and traditional art forms. The platform also allows users to host and share their custom models.

TensorArt is a free AI art generator, making it accessible to a wide audience, from beginners to experienced artists, with no subscription or payment required.

KitsAI

KitsAI is an AI voice generation platform tailored for the music industry, focusing on providing licensed AI vocals and prioritizing ethical practices in voice conversion.

Offers tools for creating or training AI voices, with a library of studio-quality, royalty-free voices suitable for various musical applications including lyric generation and sound sourcing.

Collaborates with artists for official voice licensing, enabling artists to create, license, and share their AI voice models, promoting ethical AI use in music and new opportunities for artist engagement and monetization.

KitsAI has a free plan that renews every month, you can purchase a plan for prices ranging from $10 to $60
